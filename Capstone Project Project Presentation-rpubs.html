Applied Methods & Models

The sample data obtined from the HC Corpora data site was cleaned by converting to lowercase, removing punctuation, links, white space, numbers and special characters.

Basic exploratory data analysis was done on input files. The data sample was then tokenized into n-grams.

In the fields of computational linguistics and probability, an n-gram is a contiguous sequence of n items from a given sequence of text or speech. Aggregated uni, bi and trigram frequency matrices have been transferred into frequency dictionaries.

Finally, the resulting data frames are used to predict the next word based on the text input by a user.
